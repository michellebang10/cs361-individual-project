Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do you set the time for the timer?

A: You can set the time for the timer by inputting in your desired time in the input box below the timer and clicking set.

Q: Why won't it accept the time I put for the timer?

A: Please double check the format of your input. Accepted formats include: 00:00, 0000, 000, 00, and 0. Any other format would not work.

Q: How do I add items to the to-do list?

A: Input in your item in the input box below the to-do list and click the add button.

Q: How do I delete items from the to-do list?

A: Click on '[x]' next to the item you want to remove. You will be asked to double check your choice on deleting your item as you will not be able to undo your choice.
