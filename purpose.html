MOTIMER

PURPOSE

MOTIMER is a combination of the words motivation and timer. The purpose of this application is to allow the user to efficiently manage their tasks and time and also be motivated to do their work.

FEATURES

  • Clock: Keep track of time by looking at current time down to seconds!
  • To-Do List: Keep track of your tasks by adding and deleting items from your personalized To-Do List!
  • Timer: Manage your time by using a timer! You're able to customize the length of time to your needs.
  • (COMING SOON!) Image: Motivate yourself by customizing the image you see on the main page!
